Kemia presents itself as a innovative and empowering skincare brand, that's passionate about science and honest in its use of ingredients and materials. These values are put on display via a clear and minimalist, yet sheerly colorful design, that takes direct inspiration from the periodic table and the graphical expression of oxidation numbers.

In line with the brand's values of honesty and empowerment, the oily skincare family "The Shimmer" transforms the shine of these skin types into an emblem of positivity and acceptance. Its four products are distributed across a daily, step-by-step routine, unified under the same graphic formula.
