Ephemeral Lab is an educational event that connects experts with the general public to discuss the future of our cities, acting as a hub for innovation and discourse on how cities should adapt to our current crises.

The event's design revolves around the figure of a torn fabric, through which a restorative thread is weaved, metaphorizing the current state of our cities and how cooperation and togetherness can act as a tool for change.
