ETCÉTERA is a fictional contemporary art gallery that seeks to break the intellectual barriers between artists, artworks and audiences, by creating an intersectional dialogue where all parties are put on an equal playing field. Artists create for the sole purpose of creating, and audiences can be capable of enjoying their creations without the need of a higher intellectual effort or understanding.

We decided that the gallery's identity would be centered around its ever-changing and unpretentious nature, mixing different kinds of media to create a dynamic visual system that could sustain both noisy, untidy designs as well as more minimal and clean displays of the brand's behavior.
